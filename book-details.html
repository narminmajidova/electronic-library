About This Book

The Great Gatsby is a novel of the Jazz Age that has been acclaimed by generations of readers. The story of the mysteriously wealthy Jay Gatsby and his love for the beautiful Daisy Buchanan is a tragic tale of American idealism and obsession. Set in the summer of 1922, the novel takes place following the end of World War I. The era of the story, known as the Jazz Age, was a time of great wealth and material excess, with stock market investments that seemed foolproof. It was also a time of tremendous moral decay.
